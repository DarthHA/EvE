Use the Creature Marker to make two enemies or even the bosses fight with each other!
Note: This mod might seriously affect the normal progress of the game, or even crash the game. Of course I am trying to avoid this to happen!
It is recommended to play it with Hero's Mod or Cheat Sheet Mod.
Pure entertainment mod, have fun!

-Fixed the issue about Old One's Army enemies.

-After the death of the marked creature, its minions can be marked again.
v0.4
